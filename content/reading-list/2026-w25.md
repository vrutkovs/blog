# Software Engineering & Cloud

* [VictoriaMetrics vs Prometheus: my default, and when I still pick Prometheus](https://jorijn.com/en/blog/victoriametrics-vs-prometheus/)
  
  VictoriaMetrics makes Kubernetes monitoring boring in useful ways: lower RAM, simpler HA, built-in long-term storage, and better cardinality tolerance without adding Thanos or Mimir. Prometheus still wins for healthy existing stacks, strict neutrality, or maximum PromQL portability.

  Tags: Monitoring, VictoriaMetrics, Prometheus, Kubernetes, Cardinality
